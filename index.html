Lilac Valley Farm Stay homepage

Luxury accommodation in the Blue Mountains

About

Simple, rustic farm stay accommodation in the hills of the Blue Mountains.

The property is owned by Marina Ye, a local interior designer who purchased the old rundown barn three years ago with a plan to renovate. After years of hard work, the property has been beautiful restored. Whether you’re a Sydney resident or travelling from afar, Lilac Valley Farm Stay is the perfect option for a relaxing getaway, in an amazing location.

Features

To ensure a comfortable and memorable stay, we have everything you need.

  • Beautifully restored, open plan, luxury barn house.
  • Comfortable super king sized bed.
  • On four acres of farm land.
  • Home to sheep, chickens, ducks and a goat.
  • Outdoor plunge pool just off the back veranda.
  • Stunning cottage garden.
  • Luxury amenities, inlcluding a modern kitchen.
  • Close to bushwalking and hiking trails.
